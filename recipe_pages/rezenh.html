"Rezeň"

Source: https://pxhere.com/

This delicious meat meal is a staple of Slovak and Czech kitchen! It's usually served with potatoes, or potato salad.

This recipe was obtained from this site.>

Ingredients

  • Chicken or pork meat 1 kg
  • Eggs 3 pieces
  • All-purpose Flour 8 tablesp
  • Breadcrumbs 12 tablesp
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. "We slice around 1-2cm thick slices, tenderize them and salt and pepper them.
  2. We prepare our coatings - put flour on one plate and breadcrumb on another. Beat the eggs in another deep plate, and add a dash of salt. For taste you can add a bit of beer as well.
  3. Coat the meat slices in the coats in order of flour, eggs and breadcrumbs. If coated you should work with them as soon as possible.
  4. Add oil to a pan, about 2 cm high, let it heat up properly and slowly add the meat slices. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
